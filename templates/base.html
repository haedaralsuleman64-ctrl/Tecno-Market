About Our Store

Our Story

TEcNO Market was founded in 2025 with a vision to provide the latest technology products at competitive prices. We started as a small local store and have grown into a trusted online destination for tech enthusiasts.

Our Mission

To make cutting-edge technology accessible to everyone by offering high-quality products, exceptional customer service, and competitive pricing.

Our Values

  • Quality: We carefully select all our products to ensure they meet our high standards.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Your satisfaction is our top priority.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek the latest and most innovative products.
  • Integrity: We believe in transparent and honest business practices.

Return Policy

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns within 7 days of receipt for most items in their original condition:

  • Product must be unused and in the original packaging
  • All accessories and documentation must be included
  • Product must be in resalable condition
  • Proof of purchase is required

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Products without their original packaging
  • Damaged products
  • Products marked as final sale

3. Return Process

To return an item:

  1. Contact our customer service team to initiate the return process
  2. Package the product securely in its original packaging
  3. Include the original invoice and reason for return
  4. Ship the package to the address provided by our customer service team

4. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • Refunds will be processed within 1-4 business days
  • The refund will be issued to the original payment method
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable
  • Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the return is due to our error

5. Exchanges

We are happy to exchange items for a different kind or color, subject to availability. Please contact our customer service team to arrange an exchange.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you.
